2016 Heiser Farms Cyclocross / GPCM #5 / JCS

Part of the GPCM
September 30th, 2017

Course Description:
Heiser Farms, located in Dayton, Oregon, has quickly become a favorite cyclocross venue. The farm offers a montage of terrain, the famous shoe stealing BOG, stunning vistas of the Willamette Valley and family farm fun. Heiser Farms will be open to the public for pumpkin gatherin’, launchin’, hay ridin’, corn mazin’ and goat pettin’ as well as the Half Penny Beer Garden being added this year.
