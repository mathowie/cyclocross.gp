2011 Heiser Farms Cyclocross / GPMC #3

Part of the GPMC and the Oregon Junior and High School Cross Series
October 8th, 2010

Course Description:
Heiser Farms, located in Dayton, Oregon, has quickly become a favorite cyclocross venue. The farm offers a montage of terrain, the famous shoe stealing BOG, stunning vistas of the Willamette Valley and family farm fun. Heiser Farms will be open to the public for pumpkin gatherin’, launchin’, hay ridin’, corn mazin’ and goat pettin’. The Brauts & Bluegrass Festival, benefiting the Yamhill County Foodbank, will be part of the weekend‘s festivities along with a 5k cross country run on Sunday presented by X-Dog events.
